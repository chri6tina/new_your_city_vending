Micro Market FAQs

Everything you need to know about our micro market solutions

What is a micro market and how does it work?

A micro market is a self-service convenience store that uses smart kiosks and open shelving instead of traditional vending machines. Customers can browse products freely, use the self-checkout kiosk to pay, and enjoy a more natural shopping experience with fresh food options.

How much space do I need for a micro market?

Micro markets typically require 200-400 square feet of space, depending on the size and product selection. We can design a micro market to fit your available space, from small break rooms to large employee cafeterias. Our team will assess your space and recommend the optimal layout.

What types of products can I offer in a micro market?

Micro markets can offer a wide variety of products including fresh sandwiches, salads, fruits, yogurt, beverages, snacks, and even hot food items. The open format allows for refrigerated and frozen products, making it much more versatile than traditional vending machines.

How do customers pay in a micro market?

Customers use our smart self-checkout kiosk that accepts credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments, and employee ID cards. The system is secure and user-friendly, with a touchscreen interface that makes checkout quick and easy.

How do you prevent theft in a micro market?

Our micro markets include security cameras, weight sensors, and smart inventory tracking. The self-checkout system requires payment before items can be taken, and our monitoring systems can detect unusual activity. Most locations experience very low theft rates due to the professional environment.

What's the revenue potential compared to vending machines?

Micro markets typically generate 50-100% more revenue than traditional vending machines due to the wider product selection, fresh food options, and more natural shopping experience. The higher average transaction value and increased customer satisfaction lead to significantly better performance.

How much does a micro market cost in NYC?

Costs vary by size and product mix. We offer flexible options from starter setups to full enterprise markets, with monthly fees that often include equipment, restocking, and support. Contact us for a free consultation and a quote tailored to your NYC location.

Do you install micro markets in Brooklyn and Queens?

Yes. We install and service micro markets across all five boroughs—Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island. Same-day response and regular restocking no matter where your facility is in New York City.
