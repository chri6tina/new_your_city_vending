Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our vending machine services in NYC

Is installation really free?

Yes. We provide the machine, installation, and setup at no cost to you. We operate on a revenue-sharing model so we both benefit from sales.

What payment methods do your machines accept?

All our machines accept major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and contactless payments. Select machines also accept cash.

How often do you restock?

We typically restock weekly and use remote monitoring to track inventory. High-traffic locations can be serviced more often so products are always available.

Do you serve Manhattan and Brooklyn?

Yes. We install and service vending machines in all five boroughs—Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island—with the same free placement and 24/7 support.

Can I choose what products go in the machine?

Absolutely. We work with you to curate a selection that fits your audience—healthy options, local brands, dietary needs, and seasonal items.

What if the machine breaks?

We offer 24/7 technical support and aim to repair issues within 24 hours. Our machines are monitored remotely so we often catch and fix problems before you notice.
