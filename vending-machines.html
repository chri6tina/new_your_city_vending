Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our vending machine services

How much does it cost to install a vending machine?

Installation is completely free! We provide the machine, installation, and setup at no cost to you. We make money through a revenue-sharing model where we split the profits from sales.

What types of payments do your machines accept?

Our machines accept all major credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and contactless payments. We also offer cash options on select machines.

How often do you restock the machines?

We typically restock machines weekly, but we use remote monitoring to track inventory in real-time. If a machine runs low on popular items, we'll restock it more frequently to ensure your employees always have access to fresh products.

What happens if the machine breaks down?

We provide 24/7 technical support and maintenance. If a machine breaks down, we'll repair it within 24 hours. Our machines are also monitored remotely, so we often know about issues before you do and can fix them proactively.

Can I choose what products go in the machine?

Absolutely! We work with you to curate a product selection that matches your employees' preferences and dietary needs. We can include healthy options, organic products, and specialty items based on your requirements.

Do you provide any revenue sharing?

Yes! We offer competitive revenue sharing that ranges from 60-70% depending on the service level you choose. This means you earn money from the vending machine sales while providing convenience to your employees.
