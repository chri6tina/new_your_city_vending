Pantry Service FAQs

Everything you need to know about our office pantry services

What's included in your pantry service?

Our pantry service includes premium coffee, tea, healthy snacks, beverages, and office supplies. We provide the equipment, regular restocking, maintenance, and can customize the selection based on your team's preferences and dietary needs.

How often do you restock our pantry?

We typically restock your pantry daily or weekly, depending on your usage and preferences. We monitor consumption patterns and adjust our schedule to ensure your pantry is always well-stocked with fresh products.

Can we customize the product selection?

Absolutely! We work with you to curate a selection that matches your team's preferences. We can include organic options, gluten-free products, specific coffee brands, healthy alternatives, and even specialty items based on your company culture and employee requests.

What types of coffee equipment do you provide?

We provide professional-grade coffee machines including single-serve brewers, traditional coffee makers, espresso machines, and specialty equipment. All equipment is regularly maintained and serviced to ensure optimal performance and taste.

How do you handle billing and payments?

We offer flexible billing options including monthly contracts, usage-based pricing, or flat-rate services. Payments can be made via invoice, credit card, or ACH transfer. We provide detailed reports showing consumption and costs for easy expense tracking.

What if we need to make changes to our service?

We're flexible and can adjust your service at any time. Whether you need to change product selections, adjust delivery frequency, or modify your equipment setup, we'll work with you to ensure your pantry service continues to meet your evolving needs.

What's included in office pantry service in New York?

Our NYC pantry service includes premium coffee and tea, healthy snacks, cold beverages, and office supplies. We provide equipment (brewers, dispensers), regular restocking on your schedule, maintenance, and customization for dietary and brand preferences—all available across the five boroughs.

Do you deliver pantry service to Manhattan and Brooklyn offices?

Yes. We deliver and restock pantry service to offices throughout Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island. Same reliable schedule and quality whether you're in Midtown or downtown Brooklyn.
