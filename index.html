Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our vending services

What are the costs for vending machine services?

Our pricing is flexible and depends on your specific needs. We offer competitive rates with no upfront costs for most installations. Revenue sharing options are available, and we provide detailed cost breakdowns during our consultation.

How often do you service the machines?

We provide regular maintenance and restocking based on usage patterns. High-traffic locations receive daily service, while others are serviced weekly or bi-weekly. Our smart monitoring systems alert us when service is needed.

What areas of NYC do you serve?

We serve all five boroughs of New York City: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island. Our service area covers the entire metropolitan area with quick response times.

Do you offer cashless payment options?

Yes! All our modern machines accept credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and contactless transactions. We also offer employee badge systems for corporate accounts.

How long does installation take?

Installation typically takes 2-4 hours depending on the machine type and location requirements. We coordinate with your schedule and ensure minimal disruption to your business operations.

Can you customize the product selection?

Absolutely! We work with you to curate product selections that match your audience's preferences. We can include healthy options, local brands, dietary-specific items, and seasonal products.
